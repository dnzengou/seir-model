SEIR model

Tweakable, user-friendly R app for educational simulation on complex systems dynamics, here disease spreading.
An attempt to implement the more sophisticated #SEIR model for COVID-19 pandemic to illustrate how changes in numbers of susceptibles, exposed, infected and recovered interact with each other and with ICU beds numbers.
I still need to tweak parameters and equations behind the curve, some trends might seem a bit odd... Meanwhile comments are welcomed :).
